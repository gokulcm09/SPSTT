Terms and Conditions to book and Use Mandapams under Sree Padmanabha Swami Temple Trust
Mandapams under Sree Padmanabha Swami Temple Trust is only allowed to conduct Hindu rites and rituals. Mandapams are not allowed to use for other purposes. Vegetarian meals are only allowed to cook and serve on the Mandapam. Mandapams can be booked from 10 AM to 5 P M on every working days.
1. Mandapams are only supposed to use for the purposes listed in the application form, signed while booking.
2. For booking Mandapams, you have to pay the fees and EMD assigned by the Trust and collect the receipt beforehand.
3. On the same day of booking, you have to pay the fees and EMD to the trust. Fees except EMD won't be returned at any cost. If you want the fee to be returned due to any unfortunate cancellation, then you have to notice the trust 3 months before the booking date and also the Mandapams should have any other booking on the same date. If the Mandapam hasn't got any other booking then fees won't be returned and the Trust's decision will be final.
4. Plastic products banned under the Kerala Government order (17-12-2019) is not allowed in the Mandapam premises. Hence such products are not even allowed to use for emergency purposes.
5. Things brought under written permission has to be returned as such and then report form the concerned Mandapam Authority and EMD payment receipt has to returned to Trust office in person and then paid EMD will be returned back to you. Commodities that belong to the Mandapam canot be taken outside the premises.
6. Plates and lights for emergency situations can be bought from the office after noting down in the register. Trust is not responsible for the things out of stock, Nadaswarams and for other purposes. Any complaint due to third party association wont be welcomed by the Trust authority and the concerned party will be responsible for any lose faced by the trust.
7. Mandapam, decoration, generator, cooking gas etc can be done by a contractor in the rate fixed by the trust. If you do the aforementioned works from a outside agency, then you should pay EMD of Rs. 15000 for Sree Vaikundam Mandapam and Rs. 5000 for other Mandapams and after deducting the taxes and other charges under the Royalty charges by Trust, the balance amount will be returned back to you.
8. It is not allowed to cook or use non veg items or drugs in Mandapam premises. Electric lighter, fan, pipes, furnitures etc should not be misused or damaged and also make sure to keep the premises neat. Any instructions given by mandapam authorities regarding the proceedings should be followed. Payment had to be given for using genarator for requirements like video recordings, halogen lights, serial lights, mics and so on.Crane video, helicams are not allowed in the Mandapams.
9. If multiple functions are to be conducted at the booking time , an appilication has to be given in advance and also have to pay a fixed amount. Permission must be obtained in advance by paying the extra payment for special receptions, functions, etc., on the day of the wedding or the day before, and for the erection of a separate pavilion, pandal, or shamiana outside the hall. Those who want to build a shed, pandal or shamiana have to pay a floor rent of Rs 2 per square foot in addition.
10. After the marriage,the bride and groom should write the required details and sign in the register kept in the hall.Birth certificates and address proof of the bride and groom should be handed over to the mandapam manager before the marriage.The certificate should be bought within a week and registered at the concerned office. Legally recognized names of the bride and groom should be entered . Names entered in the register will not be changed for any reason.
11. Once you have booked a Mandapam, you're not allowed to give the Mandapam to any other person for emergency situations. Mandapams can't be changed after booking.
12. Any complaints and suggestions regarding emergency uses can be noted by the applicants in the register placed in Mandapams.
13. All the instructions given by the State and Central Government is applicable to the person from the day of booking. No one has the right to violate any rules even if they had booked the Mandapam before publishing the notice from the authority if so charges will be laid from the EMD and the applicant doesn't have any right to raise any complaint against the authority.
